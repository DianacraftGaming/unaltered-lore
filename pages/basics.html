Basic Information

Explains the base story and symbolism!

The Core of the World, Order, was shattered, and everything fell into Chaos. To protect the world, three people claimed the shards of Order, acending to godhood.

But their power was too much to handle, making them lose themselves over time.

The main story follows Samael, one of the three gods, and his escape from the other two, after their ideal no longer align.
