Relationships

Kas

Samael's romantic partner! Originally Samael mainly saw Kas as person he needs to keep happy enough to not end the world while scaring away the gods of Celestia, but over time he has grown a deep trust and appreciation for Kas. Being the calmer and the more calculated one, nowadays Samael mainly acts as the voice of reason and the one to plan most of their shenanigans. The millennia spent together gave them a great understanding of each other and the ability to act as one, covering for what the other lacks.

Kas's Oracle

Samael projects onto the Oracle a lot. Both are members of helper species who got treated less than ideal by the people around them, but while Samael left that situation for a better one, Kas's oracle doesn't necessarily have the desire or ability to.
